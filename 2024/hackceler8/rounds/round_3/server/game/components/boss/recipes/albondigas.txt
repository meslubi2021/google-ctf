Meatballs

Mix some mince with onion, garlic and parsley, and pepper. Knead well by hand until the ingredients form a compact mass. With wet hands, shape the mass into balls. Fry the meatballs in batches over a medium heat, keep warm.

Tomato sauce

Heat the oil in the same pan, saute the remaining onion and garlic. Add tomatoes, season. Turn down the heat. Add the meatballs, heat gently. Sprinkle with the remainder of the parsley.
